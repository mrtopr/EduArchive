About EduArchive

A student-driven academic platform built to simplify, structure, and strengthen the B.Tech learning journey.

Why EduArchive?

Engineering students often struggle with scattered resources, unclear syllabi, outdated notes, and the constant pressure of exams and placements. EduArchive solves this by bringing everything into one reliable, structured, and easy-to-use platform.

What We Offer

Subject-wise Notes

Aligned with university syllabi and exam patterns.

PYQs

Previous Year Question Papers for focused preparation.

Placement Roadmaps

Step-by-step guidance from basics to interviews.

Structured Learning

Year-wise & semester-wise organized content.

Distraction-Free

Clean UI focused purely on learning.
